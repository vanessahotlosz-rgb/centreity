The Challenge

The Ambition Was There.
The Capability Wasn't.

UAE universities wanted to be leaders in digital learning. But without in-house expertise, infrastructure, or governance, they were locked in a cycle of expensive external dependency — unable to scale, unable to sustain, unable to own the outcome.

Centreity's Role

Implementing Partner.
Not Ongoing Vendor.

As implementing partner for AGF's UCQOL initiative, Centreity's job was to work itself out of a job — building capability so deep that universities never needed to hire us again.

Strategy & Digital Roadmaps
Revenue models, accreditation pathways, enrollment strategy tailored to each institution's context.
Faculty Development at Scale
Bootcamps, AI workshops, design sprints — 1,200+ trained with 96% satisfaction.
Studio & Infrastructure Build
Professional studios and HyFlex classrooms now fully owned and operated by each university.
Governance & Systems Embedding
Workflows, QA frameworks, and dashboards institutionalized for permanent independent use.

The Journey

Four Phases.
One Lasting Outcome.

A deliberate progression designed to move every university from external dependency to full institutional ownership — and then to a self-sustaining regional network that runs without us.

The UCQOL Model

Four Pillars That Build
Not Borrow

Every UCQOL engagement delivers across four interlocking streams — ensuring institutions build genuine capability, not ongoing dependency on external support.

01
Expert Advisory & Training
  • Tailored strategic consulting on each university's digital transformation challenges
  • Mentoring from leading experts in student-centered learning design
  • Accreditation pathways, revenue models, enrollment guidance
02
Grants for Infrastructure & Talent
  • Physical and technological infrastructure to anchor transformation
  • Talent acquisition for delivering high-quality digital programs
  • Production resources for effective, scalable implementation
03
Community of Practice
  • Monthly peer exchanges among all 11+ UAE universities
  • Shared expertise built on a culture of trust and excellence
  • Peer network that reduces duplication and accelerates outcomes
04
Regulatory & Accreditation Access
  • Streamlined navigation of accreditation and government approvals
  • Opportunities to shape national digital learning policy
  • Network-level influence on UAE regulatory frameworks
